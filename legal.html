Privacy Policy

TradeLine Marketplace LLC ("TradeLine Marketplace," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you create an account or place an order
  • Authorized user details: name, date of birth, last four digits of Social Security Number, and mailing address of the person being added as an authorized user — collected solely for AU processing
  • Payment information: cryptocurrency transaction hashes and wallet addresses, used solely to verify and confirm payment on-chain
  • Communications: emails, form submissions, and support tickets you send us

Information collected automatically

  • IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and referring URLs
  • Pages visited, time spent on site, and click patterns via analytics tools
  • Cookies and local storage used to maintain session state, cart data, and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your tradeline orders
  • To communicate with you about your orders, account, and support requests
  • To send marketing communications if you have opted in (you may opt out at any time)
  • To comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud, and enforce our terms
  • To improve our platform through aggregated, anonymized analytics

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We share your information only as follows:

  • Card holders: Authorized user information (name, DOB, SSN last 4, address) is shared with the card holder who is adding you to their account, as required to process the AU addition
  • Blockchain verification: Transaction hashes are used to verify payments on public blockchain networks. No personal financial details are shared with third parties.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or court order

4. Data Security

We use 256-bit AES encryption for all stored personal data and TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. Identity verification documents are stored in an encrypted, access-controlled vault and are accessible only to staff directly involved in processing your order.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and order information for 7 years to comply with financial record-keeping requirements. Identity verification documents are deleted within 90 days of your order's reporting period closing, unless we are legally required to retain them longer.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To submit a data request, email privacy@tradelinemarketplace.io.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and cart. We use analytics cookies to understand site usage. You may disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@tradelinemarketplace.io or write to TradeLine Marketplace LLC, 1800 Vine St Suite 220, Los Angeles, CA 90028.

1. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and a legal resident of the United States (excluding Georgia) to purchase tradelines through TradeLine Marketplace. By placing an order, you represent that you meet these requirements.

2. Nature of Service

TradeLine Marketplace facilitates the placement of customers as authorized users on third-party credit card accounts. We are not a credit repair organization as defined under the Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA). We do not guarantee any specific improvement in your credit score. Results vary based on your individual credit profile.

3. Purchase Process

  • All orders must be placed before the stated Purchase Deadline for the selected tradeline
  • Payment must clear before the AU information is submitted to the card holder
  • A valid cryptocurrency wallet and transaction hash are required to confirm payment for all orders
  • Orders are non-cancellable once AU information has been submitted to the card holder

4. Posting Guarantee

TradeLine Marketplace guarantees that each purchased tradeline will be reported to at least two (2) of the three major credit bureaus (Equifax, Experian, TransUnion) within the stated reporting period. If a tradeline fails to post to at least two bureaus, TradeLine Marketplace will issue a full credit to the customer's account. This credit must be used within 12 months.

The posting guarantee does not apply if: (a) the customer provided incorrect authorized user information; (b) the customer's credit file was frozen at the relevant bureaus during the reporting period; (c) the card issuer removed the AU due to fraud concerns related to the customer.

5. Geographic Restrictions

TradeLine Marketplace does not sell tradelines to customers whose billing address or authorized user address is located in the state of Georgia. All other U.S. states are served.

6. Payment Terms

TradeLine Marketplace accepts cryptocurrency payments (Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, Solana) and TradeLine Marketplace Store Credit only. Credit card payments are not accepted. All prices are in U.S. dollars. TradeLine Marketplace reserves the right to change pricing at any time without notice; however, the price shown at checkout is locked for your order.

Fraud Policy

TradeLine Marketplace takes fraud prevention seriously — both to protect our customers and to maintain the integrity of our platform. This policy describes how we detect, respond to, and penalize fraudulent activity.

1. Zero Tolerance for Fraud

TradeLine Marketplace operates a zero-tolerance fraud policy. Any customer, card holder, or broker found engaging in fraudulent activity will be permanently banned from the platform, forfeit all active orders and credits, and may be reported to relevant law enforcement authorities.

2. What Constitutes Fraud on This Platform

By Buyers

  • Providing false authorized user information (fake names, SSNs, or addresses)
  • Submitting fraudulent identity documents
  • Initiating cryptocurrency payment reversals or disputes for orders that were fulfilled as described
  • Using purchased tradelines to commit loan fraud or materially misrepresent creditworthiness on credit applications
  • Creating multiple accounts to circumvent bans or pricing tiers

By Card Holders (Credit Partners)

  • Listing cards that do not meet stated quality requirements
  • Accepting payment and failing to add the authorized user
  • Removing authorized users before the end of the stated reporting period without notifying TradeLine Marketplace
  • Operating cards with concealed derogatory history

By Brokers

  • Reselling at pricing that violates a signed Broker Agreement
  • Submitting orders on behalf of clients in restricted states
  • Misrepresenting TradeLine Marketplace's services or guarantees to end clients

3. Fraud Detection

TradeLine Marketplace employs automated and manual fraud detection, including: blockchain transaction verification, IP and device fingerprinting, order pattern analysis, and third-party fraud scoring. Suspicious orders may be held for manual review before processing.

4. Reporting Fraud

If you believe you have been the victim of fraud involving TradeLine Marketplace — whether by another customer, a card holder, or a broker — contact us immediately at fraud@tradelinemarketplace.io or call (310) 555-0192. Include as much detail as possible. We investigate all reports within 2 business days.

5. Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback for a fulfilled order is considered fraud. TradeLine Marketplace contests all fraudulent chargebacks and provides documentation of order fulfillment to payment processors. Customers who initiate unjustified chargebacks are permanently banned and may be pursued for the original payment plus dispute fees.

6. Contact

To report fraud: fraud@tradelinemarketplace.io · (310) 555-0192
